What is Generative Ai ?

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can create new content such as text, images, music, video, code, or even 3D models, rather than just analyzing or processing existing data. It works by learning patterns, structures, and relationships from huge amounts of training data and then using that knowledge to generate outputs that resemble human-created work.

What Ai Do ?

Automation & Productivity

AI automates repetitive tasks (data entry, customer support, scheduling).

Increases efficiency, reduces cost, and saves human effort.

Data Analysis & Insights

Processes huge amounts of data quickly.

Finds hidden patterns, trends, and predictions (used in finance, healthcare, business decisions).

Content & Creativity Generation

Creates text, images, code, music, and designs.

Helps in marketing, entertainment, education, and product development.
